Frank Ocean

The greatest artist of our generation

Frank Ocean, born Christopher Edwin Breaux on October 28, 1987, in Long Beach, California and raised largely in New Orleans, is an American singer, songwriter, and producer who rose from ghostwriting and membership in the Odd Future collective to critical acclaim with his 2011 mixtape Nostalgia, Ultra; his debut studio album Channel Orange (2012) and the follow-up Blonde (2016) expanded the boundaries of contemporary R&B with personal, genre-bending songwriting and unconventional release strategies, earning major awards, widespread influence, and a reputation as one of the most innovative and private figures in modern music.
